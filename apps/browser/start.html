Getting started

A quick tour of your new desktop, developer preview build.

Settings

Connect to Wi-Fi and Bluetooth, adjust brightness and volume, rename your device, and check for updates.

Files

Browse, copy, rename and delete files across your home folder and any mounted volumes.

Terminal

A full shell for anything the graphical apps don't cover yet.

Browser

A private-by-default browser: no history, cache or cookies survive between sessions.

First things to try

  1. Join a network
    Open Settings → Network, switch Wi-Fi on, and pick a network from the list.
  2. Look around the taskbar
    Every open app gets a button there so you can switch between them.
  3. Open the Files app
    Your home folder is at /home/outback.
  4. Pin your favourites
    Use the star in the address bar to bookmark a page for next time.
